Welcome to Harvard Summer School

On behalf of Harvard University, we extend a warm welcome to all summer school students. Harvard Summer School offers a unique opportunity to experience the vibrant academic and cultural environment of one of the world's leading universities.

As a summer school student at Harvard, you'll have access to a wide range of courses taught by distinguished faculty members. Whether you're interested in exploring new subjects or fulfilling academic requirements, the summer program offers a diverse and enriching learning experience.

Preparing for Your Summer at Harvard

To make the most of your time at Harvard Summer School, here are a few tips:

  1. Review your course schedule and familiarize yourself with the syllabus.
  2. Reach out to your professors or teaching assistants if you have any questions or need clarifications.
  3. Take advantage of the resources available on campus, such as libraries, research facilities, and academic support services.
  4. Engage with your fellow students and participate in extracurricular activities, clubs, and events to build connections and make lasting friendships.
  5. Explore the Harvard campus and surrounding areas to discover the rich history, cultural attractions, and vibrant student life.
  6. Stay organized and manage your time effectively to balance coursework, study time, and personal activities.
  7. Embrace the intellectual challenges and opportunities for personal growth that Harvard Summer School offers.

We hope you have an incredible summer at Harvard. Make the most of this transformative experience, expand your knowledge, and create lifelong memories.
